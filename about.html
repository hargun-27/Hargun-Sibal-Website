My name is Hargun, and I’m an aspiring product manager and software developer studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo.

Working and designing towards a more equitable, and ethical future


I’ve always liked to “think big”. For almost all of my high school life, I have always been a leader. In high school, I took the step to explore and take the lead in many multidisciplinary extracurriculars including DECA, Model UN, Link Crew, Bhangra, and even Hackathons.

I’m in love with connecting the dots to create something larger than the sum of its parts, and one could say that all the activites that I've done in high school tie together with my love and passion for problem solving and motivation to design products in the future. To me, solving problems are challenges I love tackling, and will embrace whenever I can because learning is a lifelong journey and it never stops.

Although Systems Design Engineering is a very broad program it is an interdisciplinary engineering field that touches upon my interests which includes software, business, and user design aspects of the engineering process, with an emphasis on big picture thinking.

Systems Design Engineering combines traditional engineering disciplines with solving complex problems through code, modelling, design, and analytical thinking, which is the perfect fit for me.
